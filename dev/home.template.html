What is DAD-IS?

DAD-IS is the Domestic Animal Diversity Information System hosted by FAO. It provides you with searchable databases of breed-related information and photos and  links to other online resources on livestock diversity. Furthermore you can find the contact information of all National Coordinators for the Management of Animal Genetic Resources. It allows you to analyse the diversity of livestock breeds on national, regional and global level including the status of breeds regarding their risk of extinction.  DAD-IS presents results in the form of attractive graphics and tables and allows for export of data.  More than 15 000 national breed populations (representing more than 8 800 breeds) from 182 countries are recorded.
